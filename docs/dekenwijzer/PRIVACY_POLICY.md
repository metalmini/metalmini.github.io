# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04-04-2026

Horse Blanket Advisor ("the App") is designed to respect your privacy and collect as little data as possible.

This Privacy Policy explains what data is used, how it is used, and what is **not** collected.

---

## 1. Data Collection

The App does **not require an account** and does not collect personal identity data.

### Data stored locally on your device
The following information may be stored locally on your device:

- Horse profile data (e.g. name, age category, coat type, clipping status)
- Housing and environment settings
- Owned blanket inventory
- Selected forecast settings (e.g. forecast time window)
- Optional saved location (if you choose to store one)

This data **never leaves your device**.

---

## 2. Weather Data

The App uses Apple's WeatherKit to retrieve weather information.

- Weather data is requested based on your selected or current location
- This request is handled through Apple's services
- The App does not store weather data long-term
- The App does not share weather data with third parties

For more information, see Apple’s privacy policy:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

---

## 3. Location Data

The App may request access to your location to provide accurate weather forecasts.

- Location access is optional
- You may choose to enter a location manually instead
- Location data is used only for fetching weather data
- Location is not tracked or stored beyond what you explicitly save

---

## 4. Data Sharing

The App does **not**:

- sell your data
- share your data with third parties
- use third-party analytics (unless explicitly added in future updates)

---

## 5. Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device using Apple's system frameworks.

There is:
- no cloud storage
- no external database
- no user account system

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the App.

If changes are made, the updated version will be available within the App or App Store listing.

## 9. Disclaimer

The App provides general guidance for horse blanket use based on input and weather data.

It does not replace professional advice or individual assessment of your horse.

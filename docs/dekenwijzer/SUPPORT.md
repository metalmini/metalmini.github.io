# Support

Thank you for using Horse Blanket Advisor.

This page provides help, troubleshooting steps, and ways to get in touch.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. The app says weather data is not available

Possible causes:

- No internet connection
- Location permissions are disabled
- WeatherKit is temporarily unavailable

Solutions:

- Check your internet connection
- Enable location access in iOS Settings
- Try again later

---

### 2. The advice seems incorrect

The app provides guidance based on:
- horse profile
- housing conditions
- weather forecast

However, every horse is different.

Always:
- check your horse physically
- monitor temperature and dryness
- adjust based on experience

---

### 3. My horse is different from the advice

That is completely normal.

You can improve the advice by:
- updating the horse profile
- adjusting sensitivity settings
- refining housing conditions

---

### 4. Can I add multiple horses?

This feature may be added in future updates.

---

### 5. Does the app store my data?

No.

All data is stored locally on your device.
No account is required.

---

## Troubleshooting

### App shows no advice
- Make sure a horse profile is created
- Check that required fields are filled in

### Weather is incorrect
- Verify your selected location
- Check location permissions
- Refresh the advice screen

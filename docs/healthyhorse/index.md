# Privacy Policy – HealthyHorse

Last updated: March 2026

HealthyHorse is designed as a privacy-first app. Your data stays on your device as much as possible.

## Data Collection

HealthyHorse does not require accounts and does not collect personal data for tracking or marketing purposes.

However, the app uses a third-party service for error monitoring.

## Error Reporting (Sentry)

HealthyHorse uses Sentry to collect anonymous error and crash reports.

This may include:
- crash logs
- device type and iOS version
- anonymized technical diagnostics

This data is used solely to improve app stability and fix bugs.

No personal data such as names, horse data, or notes are intentionally collected.

## Notifications

HealthyHorse may send local notifications to remind you of follow-up events.

- These notifications are generated on your device
- No data is sent to external servers

## Photos

If you choose to add photos:
- Images are stored locally on your device
- They are not uploaded or shared

## Face ID / Touch ID

Biometric authentication is used only to unlock the app:
- This data is handled securely by iOS
- HealthyHorse does not access or store biometric data

## Third Parties

HealthyHorse uses:
- Sentry (error monitoring)

No advertising or tracking services are used.

## Data Security

All user-entered data remains stored locally on your device.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated in the future.

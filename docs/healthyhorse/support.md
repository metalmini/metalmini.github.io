# HealthyHorse Support

Need help with HealthyHorse? You're in the right place.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### My data is gone
All data is stored locally on your device. If the app is deleted, data cannot be recovered.

### Notifications are not working
Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings:
Settings → Notifications → HealthyHorse

### Face ID or PIN not working
Try restarting the app or checking your device security settings.
